About Barbie

Barbie (Barbara Millicent Roberts) is the main protagonist of the web series Barbie: Life in the Dreamhouse. She lives in a large pink (her favorite color, duh!) mansion called the "Dreamhouse" along with her younger sisters Skipper, Stacie, Chelsea, and their pets; Blissa (fickle feline), Taffy (yellow labrador retriever), and Tawny (her noble steed). Barbie is a fashion icon, with over 135 careers, and counting. She owns her own giant boutique and is always prepared in case of a fashion emergency.

Barbie's characteristics

  1. Barbie's birthday is on the 9th of March.
  2. Barbie grew up in Willows, Wisconsin
  3. Her favorite movie is "Sad and Romantic the Sequel"
  4. Barbie is bubbly, kind, never has any bad intentions and is always ready to helps others, even when people show ill will towards her
  5. Barbie's makeup consists of dark eyeliner and light pink lipstick
  6. She loves hanging with her friends and sisters, cutting-edge fashion, acting/modeling, fan mail, being a mentor, walking in rain, dance parties, her pets, telling jokes and her long-time boyfriend Ken
  7. Her dislikes include bad attitudes, upsetting a friend, bad hair days, fashion disasters (like wearing stripes with plaid...ewww!) and losing her phone
